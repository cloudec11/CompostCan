Introducing A New Form of Energy

OUR PRODUCT

A revolutionary product

This revolutionary product, utilizes your food and household animal waste to create biogas, a reusable resource. In order to safely create clean biogas we scale down giant anaerobic digestion farms into the size of a large trash can. The safe and clean biogas is then released into a canister after a short period of time.

RESEARCH

Did you know

20% of food waste ends up in landfills. Additionally, 47% of food waste in Canada is generated in housegold waste. Now imagine if this waste could be beneficial.

USAGE

How it works

Food waste is collected over time until a certain quota is reached. Once the quota is secured, the food will begin the accelerated decomposition process. In order to safely create biogas we utilize a scaled down model of an anaerobic digester, as well as intense heat and pressure to create biogas. We then purify the biogas through chemical scrubbing and an iron sponge process.

Return Program

Introducing our return program. Beneficial for you, and beneficial for us!

How to exchange

You can contact the Worm building closest to your location, and we will come pick the canister of biogas within 1 to 2 business days. You will receive your compensation 2 days after the product is processed.

Motive

Reduce the costs for you and us. Also takes a step towards sustainability!
