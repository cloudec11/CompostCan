Introducing A New Form of Energy

OUR MISSION

Commitment to sustainable power

We want to pivot people towards a more sustainable future by introducing an alternative for electricity and heating. Using the biogas created by waste, we can utilize the gas as fuel for generators or furnaces.

REVOLUTIONARY

Innovative Design

We created an accesscible container for the storage and use of the fuel. By collecting the biogas using pipes, we can divert the gas into a compressor and purifier to then store the useful gas into easy to use canisters.
