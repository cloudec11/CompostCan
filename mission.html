Our Mission

Company Description

Worm is looking to enter the green industry, which happens to be one of the fastest growing industries of our generation. Worm takes full advantage of recent studies performed by prestigious universities such as Cornell, and applies them on a domestic scale, allowing for the concept of clean energy to be applicable to apartments and potential households all across Canada.

Problem statement

Every year roughly 20% or 11 million tonnes of food produced in Canada annually becomes avoidable food waste. Though many options exist, such as composting, many people don't perform these tasks as they are too lazy or do not have a reason to do so. According to studies, 20% of the national methane emissions comes from decomposition of food waste within landfills. In 2018, 1,401 kilotonnes of methane were generated, 418 kt were recovered and 885 kt of methane (equivalent to 22 Mt CO 2) were emitted. So how can we stop this?
